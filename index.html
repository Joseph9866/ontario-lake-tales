Discover the fascinating world of Ontario's native turtle species and learn how you can help protect these vital members of our aquatic ecosystems.

Explore Turtle Species

Why Turtles Matter

Turtles play crucial roles in maintaining healthy aquatic ecosystems across Ontario. As both predators and prey, they help balance food webs, and their movement through habitats aids in seed dispersal and nutrient cycling.

Unfortunately, many of Ontario's turtle species are now at risk due to habitat loss, road mortality, and other human-related threats. This website serves as an educational resource to raise awareness and promote conservation efforts.
